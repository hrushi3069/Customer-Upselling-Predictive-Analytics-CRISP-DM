# Customer-Upselling-Predictive-Analytics-CRISP-DM
Predictive analytics case study using CRISP-DM for customer segmentation and targeted email marketing.


## Overview

This project was completed as part of the MSc Business Analytics Strategy and Practice module at the University of Brighton.

The objective was to design a predictive analytics solution for a UK online fashion retailer (BOB) to identify customers who are most likely to purchase premium products through targeted email marketing campaigns.

Rather than sending premium product promotions to every customer, the proposed solution uses customer behaviour and engagement data to improve campaign effectiveness, customer satisfaction, and revenue.

---

## Business Problem

BOB has approximately 5 million registered customers.

Most customers purchase products within their normal spending range, making it difficult for the company to increase revenue through premium product sales.

Sending premium offers to all customers can result in:

- Low conversion rates
- Customer frustration
- Increased unsubscribe rates
- Poor marketing ROI

The goal is to identify customers who are most likely to respond positively to premium product recommendations.

---

## Business Objective

Develop a predictive analytics framework that helps BOB:

- Increase premium product sales
